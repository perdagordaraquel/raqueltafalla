Information of the song

  • The song is a pivotal moment in The Greatest Showman, performed by the fictional Swedish opera singer Jenny Lind, portrayed by actress Rebecca Ferguson.
  • The singing voice is not Ferguson's, but that of American singer Loren Allred. Ferguson has publicly praised Allred's vocal performance.
  • The music and lyrics were written by the acclaimed duo Benj Pasek and Justin Paul, who also wrote the songs for La La Land and the Broadway musical Dear Evan Hansen.
  • The song was released on December 8, 2017, as part of The Greatest Showman: Original Motion Picture Soundtrack.
